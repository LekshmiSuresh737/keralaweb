About Thrissur

Thrissur, also known as the cultural capital of Kerala, is renowned for its religious festivals, cultural heritage, and historical landmarks. It is home to several temples, churches, and mosques, showcasing the religious diversity of Kerala.

The Thrissur Pooram, an annual Hindu festival held at the Vadakkunnathan Temple, is one of the most famous festivals in Kerala, attracting thousands of visitors.
