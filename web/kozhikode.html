About Kozhikode

Kozhikode, historically known as Calicut, is a coastal city in the state of Kerala. It was a significant trading port during the medieval period and played a crucial role in the spice trade between Kerala and the rest of the world.

Today, Kozhikode is known for its serene beaches, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.
